    Summary

    Alien Shepherd is a platformer with a flying mechanic that can be activated by fusioning with a pet Jellyfish.

    Description

    You lost a sheep and must find it before the sun rises.

    On your way, you will meet a flying jellyfish that will help you in your quest. You will go through challenging levels and understand why the world is filled with mushrooms and deadly robots.

    For the courageous, some star shards can be found in the world. But be assured that they are not easy to reach.

    Features

    • More than 50 different and challenging levels with increasing difficulty

    • Two game modes (normal/hard) to please casual and hardcore players

    • Oldschool handcrafted pixel-art

    • A unique mechanic that allows flying in any direction for a short period of time

    • A cute pet jellyfish
